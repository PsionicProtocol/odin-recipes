European Bread Loaf

Description:

This is a classical done European bread loaf

this bread's rich history is only outclassed by how crispy it is!

Ingredients:

  • Unbleached Bread Flour
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Instant Yeast
  • Salt
  • Lukewarm Water
  • Yellow Cornmeal

Steps:

  1. Stir all ingredients together in bowl until the dough is roughly mixed.
  2. Kneed the dough until it is bouncy and smooth.
  3. Place the dough in a lightly greased bowl, cover and let rise for 2 hours.
  4. Deflate dough, cut it in half, and shape into ovals.
  5. Place the loaves on baking sheet covered with cornmeal
  6. Let rise then dust with flour and make four diagonal cuts along each loaf.
  7. Bake bread for 25 minutes at 450F
  8. Remove bread from rack and enjoy!
