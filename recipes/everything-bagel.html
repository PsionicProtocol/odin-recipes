Everything Bagel

Description:

This is a traditional bagel recipe that is baked with a mix of toppings

best enjoyed with a vegetable spread, this bagel recipe is sure to please!

Ingredients:

For the bagel itself:

  • Instant yeast
  • Bread flour
  • Egg wash
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Vegetable Oil

For the seasoning:

  • Poppy seeds
  • Sesame seeds
  • Onion flakes
  • Garlic powder
  • Sea salt

Steps:

  1. Wisk the yeast and warm water and add in the flour, salt, and vegetable oil.
  2. Kneed for 25 minutes and let it rise for two hours.
  3. Divide the dough into 12 equal pieces and shape the bagels.
  4. Boil bagels in water and dry, apply egg wash with brush.
  5. Sprinkle seasoning mixture over each bagel.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until the bagels have begun to brown.
  7. After letting them cool, enjoy with your favorite spread and toppings!
