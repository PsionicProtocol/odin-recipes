Oatmeal Cookies

Description:

These cookies are hearty and healthy

full of flavor and with the addition of chinnamon and nutmeg,

they're a true classic in any household!

Ingredients:

  • Margarine or Butter
  • Brown sugar
  • Granulated Sugar
  • One Egg
  • Vanilla
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Ground Cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Ground Nutmeg
  • Oats

Steps:

  1. Heat oven to 375Â°F, In large bowl, beat margarine and sugars until creamy.
  2. Add egg and vanilla; beat well. Add combined flour, baking soda, cinnamon, salt and nutmeg; mix well.
  3. Add oats; mix well. Drop dough by rounded tablespoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  4. Bake 10 to 11 minutes for a crisp cookie.
  5. Cool 1 minute on cookie sheets; remove to wire rack. Cool completely
  6. Enjoy! To store, put in tightly covered containter.
